**Last updated: March 2026**

This privacy policy explains how agentguides.dev ("the Site") collects, uses, and handles information when you visit and use the Site. We take privacy seriously and aim to be transparent about our practices.

## What Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

The Site does not require registration or account creation. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly via email.

If you send us a message or question by email, we store that correspondence only for the purpose of responding to you. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your email with third parties.

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, certain information is collected automatically by third-party services we use:

**Log data**: Your browser may send information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and the pages you visit. This data is used to diagnose technical problems and understand general traffic patterns.

**Cookies**: Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. Third-party services embedded on the Site (such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics) use cookies to function. These cookies are set by those third parties, not by agentguides.dev directly. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Google AdSense

This Site participates in Google AdSense, a service provided by Google LLC. Google AdSense serves advertisements to help fund the development and maintenance of free content on this Site.

Google AdSense uses cookies to display ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing history. If you prefer not to see personalized ads, you can opt out through Google's Ad Settings at https://www.google.com/settings/ads or by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Google's privacy policy describes how Google collects and uses information when you see or interact with ads. You can read Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

The Site's Google AdSense publisher ID is `ca-pub-7347042268747630`, which you can verify in the site's ads.txt file.

## Analytics

The Site may use Google Analytics or similar analytics tools to understand how visitors use the Site. Analytics data includes information such as which pages receive the most traffic, how long visitors stay on a page, and what geographic regions visitors come from. This information is collected in aggregate and does not identify individual users.

Analytics cookies can be controlled or disabled through your browser settings. When you disable analytics cookies, the analytics service will not record your visit, but all Site content will still be accessible to you.

## Third-Party Services

The Site embeds content and links from third-party services, including:

- **GitHub**: Code examples and source links may point to GitHub repositories. When you follow a link to GitHub, GitHub's own privacy policy applies.
- **npm and PyPI**: Package references link to npm and PyPI. Visiting these sites subjects you to their respective privacy policies.
- **External documentation**: Links to documentation sites for LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, and other tools are subject to those sites' privacy policies.

We do not have control over the data practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

## How We Use Information

Information collected on this Site is used to:

- Serve advertisements through Google AdSense to fund free content
- Understand aggregate traffic patterns to improve the Site
- Diagnose and fix technical issues
- Respond to direct email inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or trade any information to third parties. We do not use your information for email marketing.

## Cookies and How to Control Them

Cookies used on this Site fall into these categories:

**Strictly necessary cookies**: These are required for the Site to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store any personally identifiable information.

**Analytics cookies**: Set by analytics services to help us understand traffic patterns. You can disable these in your browser settings.

**Advertising cookies**: Set by Google AdSense to serve and measure advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you when a site is setting a cookie. Note that some features of the Site may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

## Data Retention

We do not operate a user database. Any data collected automatically by third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) is subject to those services' own retention policies. Direct email correspondence is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry.

## Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for developers and technical users. It is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through contact with us, please notify us and we will delete it promptly.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws including:

- The right to know what personal data is collected about you
- The right to request deletion of your personal data
- The right to opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell personal data)
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Since the Site does not maintain user accounts or intentionally collect personal information, most of these rights are best exercised directly with the third-party services (Google, etc.) that process data from your visit. For data held by us (email correspondence), contact us directly at the address below.

**California residents** (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and delete. Contact us to exercise these rights.

**European residents** (GDPR): Our legal basis for processing any personal data is our legitimate interest in operating the Site and serving relevant content. You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data. You also have the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

## Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the Site and any information you share with us. The Site is served over HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
